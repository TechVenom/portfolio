## 🎨 DESIGN PHILOSOPHY: "CYBER-SOPHISTICATED"
The design must evoke a sense of **sovereign intelligence** and **high-stakes engineering**. It should feel like an elite terminal interface blended with a premium modern web experience.

### 1. Aesthetics & Texture
- **Theme**: Deep Space Dark (not pure black, use `#020617` or `#050505`).
- **Accents**: 
  - **Venom Green**: `#22c55e` (for growth/AI)
  - **Electric Cyan**: `#06b6d4` (for system/security)
  - **Deep Amethyst**: `#7c3aed` (for mystery/sovereignty)
- **Glassmorphism**: Use `backdrop-blur` heavily on cards and navigation to create depth.
- **Grain & Noise**: Add a subtle, high-end grain overlay to the background to give it a "physical" texture.
- **Glow Effects**: Use soft radial gradients and "box-shadow" glows on interactive elements.

### 2. Typography
- **Headings**: *Outfit* or *Syne* — Bold, wide, and futuristic.
- **Body**: *Inter* or *JetBrains Mono* (for technical blocks) — Highly readable, precise.

### 3. Motion & Micro-interactions
- **Framer Motion**: Use `staggerChildren` for entering sections.
- **Smooth Easing**: Everything should move with `[0.22, 1, 0.36, 1]` cubic-bezier (The "Apple" Ease).
- **Parallax**: Subtle background movement on scroll to create a 3D sense of space.
- **Hover States**: Cards should glow and scale slightly (`scale: 1.02`) when hovered.
